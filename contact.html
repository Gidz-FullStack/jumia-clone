Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section, or by using the tracking number sent to your email.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, Verve, bank transfers, and cash on delivery for eligible orders.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location but typically range from 2-7 business days within major cities.

Can I return or exchange my order?

Yes! You have 7 days from delivery to return most items. Visit our Returns & Refunds page for more details.

How do I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order through your account before it's shipped. Go to "My Orders" and select "Cancel Order".

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect all payment information. Your data is completely secure.
