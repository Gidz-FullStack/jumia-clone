Delivery & Returns Timelines

Learn about our return policy, timelines, and refund process

Quick Summary

Return Window: You have 7 days to return items after delivery

Refund Timeline: 10 business days for most cities

Full Refund: Get your money back with no deductions

Return Policy Overview

At Jumia Nigeria, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer an easy return and refund policy to ensure your shopping experience is hassle-free.

Eligibility for Returns

You can return most items purchased on Jumia within 7 days of delivery. To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following conditions:

  • Item must be in its original condition with all seals, tags, and accessories intact
  • Item must be in its original packaging
  • Free gifts (if any) must be returned along with the item
  • Return request must be initiated within 7 days of delivery

Valid Reasons for Returns

We accept returns for the following reasons:

  • Wrong product delivered
  • Damaged or defective product
  • Product does not match description
  • Fake or counterfeit product
  • Change of mind (for fashion items only - excludes underwear, adult toys, perfumes, cosmetics, hair extensions, and jewelry)

Non-Returnable Items

Please note that certain items cannot be returned:

  • Jumia Global items (change of mind returns not accepted)
  • Underwear and intimate apparel
  • Opened cosmetics and perfumes
  • Adult toys
  • Digital products and services
  • Fresh food items (refunds only for delivery failures)

How to Return an Item

Returning an item on Jumia is simple. Follow these steps:

1

Log into Your Account

Go to "My Orders" in your Jumia account

2

Select the Order

Click on "Order Details" to open the specific order containing the item you want to return

3

Choose Products to Return

Select the quantity to return, choose the reason for return, and add any comments to clarify your request

4

Submit Return Request

Complete the return form or call our customer service for assistance

5

Package the Item

Place the item in its original packaging with all accessories, tags, and labels

6

Return Pickup or Drop-off

Our delivery agent will pick up the item, or you can drop it off at one of our pickup stations

Start a Return Request

Return and Refund Timelines

Return Pickup Timeline

The return pickup time starts from the day you make a request to return an item to the day our delivery agent retrieves the item. This typically takes 1-5 business days.

Refund Processing Timeline

The refund time starts from the day the item is picked up by our delivery agent to the day the refund process is completed.

Important Note

If the reason for returning an item cannot be validated through our quality check process, the item will be redelivered to you and a refund will not be processed.

Refund Methods

We offer the following refund options for returned items:

  • Store Credit: Instant credit to your Jumia account for future purchases
  • Bank Transfer: Direct refund to your bank account (takes 2-5 days to reflect)
  • Mobile Money Transfer: For customers using mobile money services

Full Refund Guarantee: For prepaid orders, you will receive a full refund with no deductions when your return is approved.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I return an item after 7 days?

You will not be able to return an item after 7 days. However, if the item is faulty, it may be covered under warranty. Visit our warranty page for more information.

What happens if I miss the pickup attempts?

If you miss both pickup attempts, your return request will be cancelled and you will no longer be able to return that item.

Can I replace or exchange an item instead of getting a refund?

At the moment, Jumia does not offer replacement or exchange of items. However, you can return an item and receive a full refund, then place a new order for the correct item.

Do I have to return free gifts?

Yes, any free gifts or promotional items received with your order must also be returned along with the main product.

I haven't received my bank transfer refund. What should I do?

Bank transfers typically take 2-5 days to reflect in your account. If you haven't received your refund after this period, please contact our customer service team.

Why was my item redelivered instead of refunded?

Returned items will be redelivered to you if we cannot verify or validate your reason for returning it during the quality check process.

Quality Check Process

All returned items undergo a quality check to verify the reason for return. This process ensures that:

  • The item is in its original condition
  • All accessories and packaging are included
  • The stated reason for return is valid
  • The item has not been damaged by the customer

Contact Customer Service

If you have any questions about returns or need assistance with your return request, our customer service team is here to help:

  • Phone: Call our customer service hotline
  • Live Chat: Chat with us directly on the website
  • Contact Form: Submit a request through our contact form
  • Email: Send us an email with your order details
